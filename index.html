About Us

A talented and dedicated team of chefs and food researchers who live by the principles of creativity, originality and quality.

We provide great services

Fondly known as “The Historian Chef” or “Chef Who Wears Many Hats”, Michael Swamy is a pioneer in the area of food media and is a rare combination of chef-food stylist-food photographer-author. A graduate of the prestigious Le Cordon Bleu, London, he has trained under several Michelin chefs and over time, has evolved his signature cooking style best described as “technique-based cooking using beverages”.

The brain behind many a restaurant and culinary establishments across India, Michael, with his team, has conceptualised and handled major food shows on several GEC and international niche channels. He has authored several cookbooks and is a four-time Gourmand Award winner.

A firm believer in the power of mastering technique, he is making an effort towards introducing a fresh teaching methodology based on technique and visual content which will enable hospitality students to develop a skill set suitable for global standards. A keen passion for nature and wildlife has led Michael to creating culinary experiences that combine food and nature and bring out the interconnection between them.

Books

Books written by Michael Swamy

MASALA DABBA

The Indian kitchen is incomplete without the masala dabba or the spice box that comprises a world of flavours, colours and textures. Masala Dabba presents a melange of culinary techniques and unique spice blends and mixtures that would guarantee a delightful dining experience.
Publisher : OM Books International

THE EAST INDIAN KITCHEN

This book is a collection of Indian regional dishes that are never seen in the limelight despite being very popular in their regions. A collection of the East Indian community, the dishes are a mix of Indo Portuguese, Anglo Indian and Anglo French
Publisher : Westland Books.
Gourmand Award Category : Best Indian cookbook

EASY GUIDE TO PAIRING INDIAN FOOD AND WINE

Surprise, surprise! Indian food and wine do go together and The Easy Guide to Pairing Indian Food and Wine tells you how. While matching food with a particular wine is a matter of personal taste, this guide book not only explains the nuances of wines but also has recipes made using wine helping one enjoy a new facet of the beverage.
Publisher : OM Books International.
Gourmand Award Category : Pairing Food & Beverage

COMFORT FOOD

By Michael Swamy & Mugdha Savkar From exploring the spicy flavours of Moroccan food to indulging in Italian culinary treats that go beyond the usual pizzas and pastas; Comfort Food offers an eclectic mix of traditional and modern cuisine guaranteeing to soothe the evolving Indian palate today.
Publisher : OM Books International.
Gourmand Award Category : Easy Recipes
